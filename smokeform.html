Hunting Horror vs. Miko: Defenses in Action

Exploring the interplay of defenses and attacks

A Hunting Horror’s Layered Defenses

A Hunting Horror’s layered defenses make it tough for Miko to land meaningful hits. Key abilities include:

  • Smoke Form: Grants incorporeality, which includes a +5 AC bonus (from Cha), and a 50% chance to ignore damage, even when hit with magic weapons. This uses the Monster Manual III rules, which were intended to supersede the original rules. Rich has a design credit in Monster Manual III, likely familiarizing him with the updated rules for incorporeal creatures. This makes their use here reasonable.
    In its smoke form, attacks could harmlessly swirl through the Hunting Horror, fitting the Monster in the Dark’s laughter and claim that it tickles.
  • Deflect Harm: Negates any hit at the cost of 2 Int damage. If used, it overrides Smoke Form’s ability to ignore damage, but lets the Hunting Horror keep the +5 AC bonus. While Deflect Harm’s requirements for activation and concentration make it less fitting for the Monster in the Dark’s portrayal, it remains a plausible alternative and is included for consideration.

Defense Breakdown

  • 50% miss chance from being concealed in darkness.
  • AC 26: 10 +9 natural, +5 deflection, +3 Dex, -1 Large size
  • 50% chance to ignore damage when hit, from being in an incorporeal smoke form.
  • Fast Healing 10: Renders the first 10 points of damage each round moot.

Likely Outcomes

Based on reasonable assumptions and AI-generated math, we can calculate Miko’s odds of overcoming these defenses and causing damage:

  • 80% chance Miko does no damage in any given round.
  • 10% chance she deals 5 or less damage after fast healing.
  • 10% chance she does 6 or more damage after fast healing.

Furthermore, Miko is unlikely to be able to keep up with Fast Healing 10 when most of her shots do 0 damage. Rich’s portrayal of the Monster laughing and saying, "It tickles," matches Miko’s attacks often doing no damage and rare hits being quickly healed.
