#  O-Chul

## Paladin 4

In [Strip 1335](https://www.giantitp.com/comics/oots1335.html), O-Chul states that he has healing powers. However, it is established earlier that [Charisma is his dump stat](https://www.giantitp.com/comics/oots0545.html).

A Paladin’s **Lay on Hands** ability heals a number of hit points equal to the Paladin’s level × Charisma bonus. Because there is no minimum healing amount, a Paladin with no Charisma bonus cannot use the ability.

Paladins gain spellcasting at **level 4**, provided they have **Wisdom 12 or higher**. At that point they can cast low-level Cleric spells such as *Cure Light Wounds*, which would account for O-Chul’s statement.

At **level 5**, a Paladin gains a **Special Mount**. O-Chul is never shown with a Special Mount.

Taken together, these details imply that O-Chul has **exactly four Paladin levels**.
