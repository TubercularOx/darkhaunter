## Nale — Least Devil (1322–1338)

**Nale** is a **Least Devil**, empowered by externally supplied **soul energy**.

### Status
- [Promoted into a devil by the IFCC](https://www.giantitp.com/comics/oots1322.html)
- [Explicitly expects to become a devil and later identifies himself as “the least devil”](https://www.giantitp.com/comics/oots1326.html)
- [Manifests physical devil traits (horns)](https://www.giantitp.com/comics/oots1326.html)
- [Displays devil-typical environmental tolerance (extreme cold)](https://www.giantitp.com/comics/oots1327.html)

## Soul Energy (Fountain of Overriding Diminishment)

- [Targets a single linked fiend](https://www.giantitp.com/comics/oots1324.html)
- [Enhances fiendish attributes and fiendish magic powers](https://www.giantitp.com/comics/oots1324.html)
- [Fueled by siphoned soul energy from damned souls across the Lower Planes](https://www.giantitp.com/comics/oots1324.html)
- [Wasteful but effective](https://www.giantitp.com/comics/oots1324.html)
- [All demonstrated effects apply only to fiends](https://www.giantitp.com/comics/oots1324.html)
